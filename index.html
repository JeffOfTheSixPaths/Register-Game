    Instructions

    Goal: Turn all slots into 1.

    • Flip or shift the register using the given move buttons.
    • You have to use the least number of moves to achieve the goal.
    • The answer is the sequence of moves used as a number without spaces.
    • For example, if the solution is Move 4 followed by Move 3, enter "43".
    • Hint: the solution is 4 numbers long.
